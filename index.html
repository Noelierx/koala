Meet Koala

Koala is a macbook case on which you can write and erase endlessly. It is a portable whiteboard that protects your computer in spite of a shock. Made in France from recycled plastic, you are making an ecological gesture and supporting the French industry by purchasing this product.

If this product is called Koala, it's because it also aims to help save these marsupials after the fires that took place in Australia.

Our product has been specially designed for designers, freelancers, but also for speakers and students.
