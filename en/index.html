What is it? -

Presentation of Koala

A protective case on which you can write? It's now possible thanks to Koala! Thinked and designed in France, this case is made from recycled plastic bottles in a workshop in Rennes.
In addition to being 100% made in France and ecological, Koala is committed to donating part of its revenues to the association Queensland Koala Crusaders to help koalas thrive again in this region of Australia.
